What to write about

  • Feedback & ideas — tell me what's useful, what's confusing, or which tool you wish existed.
  • Bug reports — if a tool misbehaves, let me know your browser and device, and what you were trying to do. It helps me fix it faster.
  • Partnerships & enquiries — happy to hear from people interested in working together.

A quick note on your files

Because every tool runs entirely in your browser, your documents are never uploaded and I never receive them. So if something goes wrong with a specific file, please don't email me the document — just describe what happened, and I'll help from there.
